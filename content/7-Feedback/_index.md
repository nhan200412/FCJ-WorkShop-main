# Feedback & Suggestions

After completing the 12-week AWS Cloud Workshop, I would like to express my sincere appreciation to the organizers, mentors, and all the members who supported us throughout the internship. Below are some personal feedback and suggestions that I hope will help improve future training programs.

---

### 1. Positive Aspects of the Program

* **Practical learning experience:** The workshop focused heavily on hands-on labs rather than theory, allowing participants to work directly with AWS services such as VPC, EC2, IAM, S3, Lambda, RDS, and AI/ML. This approach helped me gain valuable practical experience.

* **Supportive learning environment:** Whenever I encountered technical issues during the labs, the mentors and administrators provided timely guidance and clear explanations, making it easier to overcome challenges and continue learning.

* **Meaningful project experience:** The group project gave us an opportunity to apply what we had learned by designing and demonstrating a Serverless Backend architecture on AWS. This experience improved both my technical knowledge and teamwork skills.

---

### 2. Challenges Encountered

* **Large amount of new knowledge:** During the first few weeks, it was challenging to understand many new AWS concepts and services at the same time. It required additional self-study to fully grasp the learning materials.

* **Service limitations:** Some AWS services and sandbox account restrictions limited access to advanced features, making it difficult to fully explore certain real-world scenarios.

---

### 3. Suggestions for Future Programs

* **Provide more guided hands-on sessions:** Organizing additional step-by-step workshops at the beginning of the program would help new participants become familiar with AWS services more quickly.

* **Offer a clearer learning roadmap:** A recommended weekly study plan, including suggested labs and learning objectives, would help participants manage their learning progress more effectively.

Overall, this internship provided me with valuable knowledge and practical experience in cloud computing. It also strengthened my problem-solving ability and encouraged continuous learning. I sincerely appreciate everyone who contributed to making this program a meaningful experience.
